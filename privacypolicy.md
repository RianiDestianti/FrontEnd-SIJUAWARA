PRIVACY POLICY – STUDENT ATTITUDE SCORING APP

Last updated: 17 January 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how this application (the “App”) handles information used for student attitude scoring/assessment.

1) Information We Process

The App processes only the information entered by authorized users (e.g., teachers or school staff), which may include:

Student information

Student name

Class/group

Attitude assessment information

Attitude scores/ratings based on rubrics (e.g., discipline, responsibility, teamwork, politeness, etc.)

Short observation notes related to attitude (optional)

Important: Please avoid entering sensitive information that is not necessary for attitude scoring (e.g., health data, religion, family matters, or other highly sensitive personal data).

2) Purpose of Use

The information is used to:

Record and calculate student attitude scores

Generate attitude assessment summaries per student and/or class

Support educational documentation and reporting

3) Data Storage and Processing

Depending on the App’s features, data may be stored in one of the following ways:

Local storage (on the user’s device): Data is stored on the device and can be removed by clearing the App’s data or uninstalling the App.

Cloud/server storage (if sync/backup is available): Data may be stored to enable synchronization and backup. Access is restricted to authorized users (e.g., teachers/admins).

4) Data Sharing

We do not sell student data or assessment data.

We do not share data with third parties except where necessary to operate the App within the school environment.

If third-party services are used for hosting/backup, they act only as service providers and are required to protect the data and keep it confidential.

5) Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as needed for educational assessment and school administration. Authorized users (teachers/admins) may delete data according to school policy.

6) Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data from unauthorized access. However, no method of storage or transmission is 100% secure.

7) Children’s Privacy

The App is used for educational purposes. Student data is entered and managed by authorized school personnel.

8) Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
